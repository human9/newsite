Abstract Submission

To submit an abstract for a poster or oral presentation, download the Abstract Template from the Monash.Shop registration site when you register.

Poster presentations

  • All posters must be in portrait format, A0 size
  • Applicants will be notified if their abstract is successful on Monday 15 October
  • Selected successful poster abstracts will be asked to present their poster in the form of a “lightning talk” before their poster session

Oral presentations

  • All presentations are limited to an 8 min talk with 2 min question time
  • Slide presentations will need to be given to an EAPS committee member at the conference
  • Applicants will be notified if their abstract is successful on Monday 1st October

Lightning talks

  • Selected successful poster abstracts will be asked to present their poster in the form of a “lightning talk” before their poster session
  • All presentations are limited to a 2 min talk with no question time
  • A single, static slide is permitted; this will need to be given to an EAPS committee member at the conference

Travel Grants

To apply for a travel grant, download and submit the Travel Grant Template from the Monash.Shop registration site when you register. Applicants will be notified if they are successful for a travel grant on Monday 1st October.
